About Holgang

Originally, Holmgang was a duel practiced by medieval Scandinavians as a way to settle their disuputes. The duels were not fought with the likes of guns but with axes and swords in an area defined by the cloak of an ox. Because these practices are barbaric, we have refined the duel to be fought in the likes of a computer screen with only your wits at your desposal. Good luck, and may the odds ever be in your favor.
